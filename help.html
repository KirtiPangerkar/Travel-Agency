Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a bus ticket?

To book a bus ticket, follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website and enter your departure and destination details.
  2. Select the date and time of travel.
  3. Choose the desired bus from the available options.
  4. Enter your personal information and make the payment.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email with your ticket details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and popular online payment methods such as PayPal.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking by logging into your account or contacting our customer support.
