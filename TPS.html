Tourism Private Sectors

Private Sector

  • Private limited company: A privately owned company with limited liability.
  • Partnership: A business owned and operated by two or more partners.
  • Limited Liability Partnership (LLP): A partnership in which partners have limited liability.
  • Sole Proprietor: A business owned and operated by a single individual.

Public Sector

  • Government controlled: Tourism entities controlled by the government.
  • Local Government controlled: Tourism entities controlled by local government bodies.
  • Membership organizations: Tourism organizations based on membership.
